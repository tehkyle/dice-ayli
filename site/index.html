First launch
  1. Open the .dmg, drag the app to Applications.
  2. Try to open the app — macOS will block it the first time. Open System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down, and click Open Anyway. Confirm in the dialog that follows. Only needed once.
  3. Click the gear icon → connect Google account → pick spreadsheet and tab. Badge turns green when ready.
Show day
  1. Open the app. Wait for the QLab status indicator to turn green.
  2. Click Begin Show.
  3. Select scenes for each act. Use Fix Order to set a specific sequence.
  4. Assign actors to each track. Conflicts highlight red. Use Randomize unfilled if needed.
  5. Review, then click Lock & Send to QLab. Green banner = QLab notified.
  6. Use the GO button (or spacebar) to advance QLab during the show. The next cue is shown above it.
  7. After the show, a row is automatically written to your Google Sheet.
